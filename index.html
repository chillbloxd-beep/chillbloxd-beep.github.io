Blackjack Tutorial

Welcome to the table. You can Play Tutorial to walk through the rules, or Skip Tutorial and start immediately.

Core Goal

  • Beat the dealer without going over 21.
  • Ace counts as 1 or 11.
  • Face cards are worth 10.

Official-style table rules implemented

  • 6-deck shoe, shuffled at ~75% penetration.
  • Dealer stands on soft 17.
  • Blackjack pays 3:2 (unless dealer also has blackjack = push).
  • Double after split allowed.
  • Split pairs up to 3 hands (4 total).
  • Split aces receive one card each only.
  • Late surrender allowed (except after split).
  • Insurance offered when dealer upcard is Ace (2:1 payout).
  • Dealer peeks for blackjack with Ace/10 upcard.

Action Buttons

  • Hit / Stand / Double / Split / Surrender / Insurance.

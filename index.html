Blackjack Royale Tutorial

Welcome to the premium table. You can walk through a quick tutorial or skip and play instantly.

Game Goal

  • Beat the dealer without busting over 21.
  • Ace is 1 or 11, face cards are 10.
  • Natural blackjack pays 3:2.

Advanced Table Features

  • 2, 3, or 4 players selectable before each round.
  • Every player starts with $1000 bankroll.
  • Extra actions: Rebet Last, All-In, New Shoe, Reset Session.
  • Dealer draws to at least 17, and natural blackjack pays extra.
